Quick Help

Dates to Book: Specific dates the bot will try to book once.
Booking Days: Regular weekdays for auto-mode booking.
Auto Generate: Creates dates for the next few weeks based on your selected days.

Priority 1 desks are tried first, then Priority 2, and so on.
Use ranges like "2.24.01-2.24.20" to specify groups of desks.
The bot will try all desks in Priority 1 before moving to Priority 2.

The bot waits longer between rounds as time progresses:
• Early rounds: Check frequently for best desks
• Mid rounds: Moderate checking pace
• Late rounds: Conservative checking to avoid server load
